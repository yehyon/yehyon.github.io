Refinery29 OINB

Reimagining OITNB characters as Disney heroines

Overview

This was a freelance project for a US online publication Refinery29. The theme was to reimagine OITNB characters as Disney heroines. I had full freedom over the concept and execution. After being published on Refinery29, it was further featured on publications such as Cosmopolitan and Glamour magazine.

Freelance Illustrator

Paper, Pencil, Water Color, Photoshop

Published in 2015

Illustrations

I first hand sketched each character on paper with pencil. Afterwards I scanned all of my sketches to digitize my work and continue the process with Photoshop. Color was added on through Photoshop.
