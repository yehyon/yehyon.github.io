Poptip Zipline

Monitor your audience chatter on Instagram & Twitter. Learn what your audience likes to talk about.

Listen to the Chatter

Poptip Questions was an eye opening opportunity to learn that how much people wanted to listen to the social media chatter, but how few ways there were to do so. Zipline was built with the intention to allow customers to filter out the noise and funnel the online chatter.

Lead Product Designer

Research, Information Architecture, Interaction, Visual design & testing

Launched in 2014

The Problem

Despite the massive increase in twitter & facebook usage, the conversation remained one way. As a poster, sharing became easier than ever but there was no real effective way to listen to one’s audience.

You can count likes and favorites, but once you get to comments it's a blackhole. Keeping up becomes impossible after a certain point.

Poptip Zipline

Zipline was a market research tool that allowed users to track social media crowd reaction through tracking text and images. The biggest challenge of designing this prodcut was making sense out of all the noise and vastness of social media data.

Zipline Detail View Live

Unique Challenges

Unlike Twitter Ziplines, Instagram Ziplines tracked two different kinds of data: hashtags and images. When the user creates an Instagram Zipline to track a certain hashtag, Poptiip Zipline would aggregate images that contain the target hashtag and also other hashtags used with the target hashtag. The Product

When a user clicks on the Zipline, one goes into the detail view. The main goal of the detail view was to allow users to see as much data possible in a single view while keeping the inflow of data as less visually jarring as possible.
