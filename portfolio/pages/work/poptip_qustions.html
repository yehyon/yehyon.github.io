Poptip Questions

A Realtime Polling App That Aggregates Crowd Voice Through Twitter & Facebook

The Desire for Instant Feedback

We want to know what people think. From the very beginning of Poptip there was a common theme, which was the desire to hear the crowd. Our first product: Questions, manifested that desire by creating a way for our users to ask a question across various social media platform and aggregate/share the crowd’s opinion.

Lead Product Designer

Research, Information Architecture, Interaction, Visual design & testing

Launched in 2012

The Problem: No Effective Way to Listen

Despite the massive increase in twitter & facebook usage, the conversation remained one way. As a poster, sharing became easier than ever but there was no real effective way to listen to one’s audience.

You can count likes and favorites, but when it comes to comments, the tally gets lost after a certain number.

Core Users

Initially we started building our product for general usage: anyone who wanted to ask a question and hear back from one’s audience. However, we quickly realized our niche was with social media power users. People and brands who have thousands and millions of followers.

Time 100, Bravo TV, CNN Crossfire, Budweiser, etc...

Pain Points

1. Our core users have the unique problem of receiving too many reactions to manually analyze.
2. It is cumbersom to manage multiple social media platform. The desire for a single platform to manage their social media activity.
3.

The Solution

Our first solution for this problem came in the form of a real-time polling app: Poptip Questions.

Poptip Questions was a social media polling app that could be used with either Facebook or Twitter. The dashboard allowed users to post a new questions or visit their past questions.

Further Evolution of Questions

We continued to evolve our product to create better voting experiences for our users. With the increase in number of influential customers, we were able to form a partnership with Twitter to design special twitter cards for enhanced voting experience for our users during special occasions.
